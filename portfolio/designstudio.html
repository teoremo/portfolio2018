Design Studio project: an agrovillage in Fornovo di Taro

Designing a self-sustainable community

  • Date: July 2014. / Category: Service design

The challenge of the course, tutored by the respected architect Aldo Cibic, was to produce responsible changes, to support improvement of human relationships and to increase a community’s quality of life through a one month-intensive research and design activity.

The project Cultivate a Community seeked to design a new typology of settlement: environmentally friendly and energetically, alimentary self-sufficient in a specific area located near Fornovo di Taro, an Italian village close to Parma.
