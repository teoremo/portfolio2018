Harika

Italian digital startup operating in the hair-styling industry

  • Date: January 2014. / Category: Branding, UI/UX design

Harika is a mobile app which aims customers to find everything about hairdressing – stylist, saloons, photos of haircuts and offered services – in a certain area. It also includes an online management section for professionists.

I’ve been asked to become part of the team for this work-in-progress startup, taking care about the art direction of the design team.
Consequently, I've been responsible for developing the project identity in every aspect (from logos to typography, colours, patterns) and then to realise the app’s user interface and all the website pages.
The app has been featured as one of the "Best new apps" in the iTunes Store
